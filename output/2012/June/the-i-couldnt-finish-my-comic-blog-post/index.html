The, I couldn't finish my comic, blog post

Published: Mon 11 June 2012
By Alistair
In:
  1. Blog
  2. Personal

Tags:
  • Health
  • Mental
  • Standup

Turns out I lied, but not for lack of good intentions. I wanted to be updating, I really did, but I was in bed sick all week with a bad fever. I'm still having trouble concentrating. This leads to the double problem of not having written anything, nor done anything to inspire writing.

Anyway, lots of rage and stuff, in the most non rage-y way. Lots to think through. (I don't like this vague sentence any more but I dislike removing it more. Needless to say my brain is hurting.)

Was drawing a comic, but I feel it might be too ambitious and not funny enough, and it definitely can't be finished today. I'll try again tomorrow. I hope to finish it, but there is a > 0% chance I hate it halfway through and give up.

Standup on wednesday. Hooray.

This feels too short.
