Day of Good

Published: Sun 27 May 2012
By admin

In Personal.

tags: JamsMusicSydneyVivid

Today was spent doing awesome things. I had my first ever musical jam with a friend of mine, we're starting a band or something. Anyways, it was fun. Reminded me of all this musical theory stuff that I have stored in the back of my brain somewhere.

Then went to the Vivid festival, which is like a festival of lights. Its artistic installations that light up. Got some awesome photos and hopefully some awesome video. Stand outs were the building used as a digital canvas to tell a story, and I really enjoyed the three angler fish mounted on tricycles that moved around Circular Quay.
